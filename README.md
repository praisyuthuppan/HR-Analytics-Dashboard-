# HR-Analytics-Dashboard-
HR Analytics Dashboard | Employee Attrition Analysis | Tableau
I’m excited to share my latest project where I built an interactive HR Analytics dashboard using Tableau to uncover insights into employee attrition and workforce behavior.
In today’s data-driven world, understanding why employees leave is just as important as hiring them. This project focuses on transforming raw HR data into actionable insights that can support better decision-making.

📊 About the Project:
Analyzed a dataset of 1470 employees across multiple dimensions.
Designed an interactive dashboard to explore attrition, job satisfaction, workforce distribution, and employee demographics.
Created key KPIs such as Attrition Rate, Active Employees, and Employee Distribution.

🔍 Key Insights:
The organization has an attrition rate of 16.12%, indicating moderate workforce turnover.
The R&D department shows the highest attrition, highlighting challenges in retaining technical talent.
Employees aged 25–34 experience the highest turnover, reflecting early-career mobility.
Male employees show higher attrition, largely due to workforce distribution.
Major job roles like Sales Executives, Research Scientists, and Laboratory Technicians dominate the workforce, with varying satisfaction levels.
Employees from Life Sciences and Medical fields contribute significantly to attrition, likely due to strong external opportunities.

💡 What This Means:
 These insights highlight how factors like age, department, job role, and education background influence employee retention. With the help of data visualization, organizations can proactively address these challenges and build stronger retention strategies.
